A space built for stillness

Our name comes from the Hebrew word sgula — a remedy, a charm — paired with the emerald, a stone prized for calm and renewal. Emerald Sgula is a home-based clinic run by a licensed massage therapist who believes recovery should feel like an escape, not an errand.

The treatment room is quiet, warmly lit, and scented with natural oils. Your therapist is certified with 5+ years of experience across Swedish, deep tissue, sports, and prenatal massage.
